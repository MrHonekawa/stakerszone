StakersZone on Bitcoin Cash smartBCH


Stake TOKEN in StakersZone

You can stake any amount of TOKEN at any time at StakersZone and claim XYZ tokens as a reward (14% of the amount staked) after 7 days.

You pay only blockchain transaction fee.
For the first time you can claim reward immediately after placing your stake (when transaction is confirmed). You need to wait 7 days to claim again.
You can claim reward later (e.g. in a month), but you will receive 14% of your balance staked. You can unstake at any time.

You must have MetaMask wallet configured for smartBCH (Bitcoin Cash side-chain) - see below.
Refresh the page if Vandals Bank is not connected to MetaMask. You may need to double-click buttons below.
